## Video direction

Use the verified GitHub Actions browser screenshots only when the video explicitly
labels them as working-prototype CI evidence. All reconstructed tabletop sequences
remain concept visualization, not gameplay capture, with a persistent edge disclosure.
Keep one dark low-poly tabletop
laboratory world across the causal loop. The evidence rail persists on the
right; the lower caption band stays clear; the upper-left disclosure remains
visible on every synthetic game-style scene. Use original HTML/CSS/SVG for the
gameplay explanation, the supplied Azure FLUX hero still only as an opening and
closing continuity anchor, and real repository file names only in the process
proof. The kaiju is cooperative, careful, and never the joke. Failure appears as
reversible toy-scale evidence, not destruction.

Palette roles come directly from `frame.md`: cream is the midnight lab canvas,
cream-2 is the slate test bench, ink is ivory type/borders, green is verified
pass, pink is regression, orange is route cyan, and yellow is helmet/current
action. Every reveal follows the spoken cue on a smooth long-tail settle; no
frame dumps its whole canvas in the opening quarter. Frames 5, 7, and 8 contain
deliberate still holds for the thesis, tiny seal, and final lockup. During holds,
prefer stillness; at most one subtle finite jitter may remain. Never use lazy
breathing, back-half camera drift, bouncy default entrances, floating independent
screensaver motion, gradients, realistic destruction, brand/device logos, fake
terminal output, or important content in the bottom caption band.
